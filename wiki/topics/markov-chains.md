# Markov Chains

## Overview

Discrete-time Markov chains model systems whose future depends on the past only through the present state. A homogeneous chain is governed by one transition matrix, making path probabilities, multi-step transitions, first-passage quantities, and absorption probabilities computable by matrix and first-step methods.
